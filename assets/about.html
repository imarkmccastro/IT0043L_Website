About CampusSecure

Our mission to strengthen cyber awareness and reporting in Philippine universities.

Our Mission

CampusSecure is a front-end prototype designed to improve cyber awareness and expedite threat reporting within Philippine universities.

With the rapid shift to digital academia, the risk of cyber attacks on colleges and universities has increased significantly. Our platform serves as a visual demonstration of how an accessible and clearly designed interface can empower students and faculty to identify threats and report suspicious activity, fostering a culture of digital safety.

We aim to simulate a centralized system that promotes vigilance and provides the tools necessary for a proactive approach to campus cybersecurity.

The Challenge We Address

Philippine universities are increasingly targeted by cyber threats, yet many lack accessible tools to respond effectively.

Research indicates that academic institutions often detect cyber attacks only after significant damage has occurred. This delay is worsened by a general lack of awareness and the absence of user-friendly reporting systems. Traditional defenses are no longer sufficient to protect the sensitive data and digital infrastructure of modern campuses.

CampusSecure addresses this gap by providing a user-centered experience that encourages early detection and clear reporting, turning every member of the campus community into a part of the defense system.
