Use this form to report any suspicious activity, such as phishing emails, strange links, or potential malware. Your report will be sent directly to the IT security team for investigation.

Tips for Reporting

Be Specific

Provide details like sender emails, full URLs, and exact wording.

Don't Engage

Do not reply to suspicious emails or click on any unknown links.

Attach Screenshots

A picture of the incident is extremely helpful for our team.
