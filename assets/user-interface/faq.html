CampusSecure is a platform designed to improve cybersecurity awareness and make it easy for students and faculty to report suspicious digital activity. It helps keep our university's digital environment safe by enabling quick communication with the IT security team.

You should report anything that seems suspicious or out of the ordinary. This includes, but is not limited to:
  • Phishing emails (emails asking for your password or personal information).
  • Suspicious links in emails, texts, or on social media.
  • Potential viruses or malware on university computers.
  • Unauthorized access to your university accounts.
  • Lost or stolen university-owned devices.
When in doubt, it's always better to report it.

Your report is not anonymous to the IT security team. Your user information is attached to the report so that our team can contact you for more details if necessary. This helps us investigate the issue more effectively. However, your identity will be kept confidential and will not be shared outside of the security investigation team.

Once you submit a report, it is assigned a case ID and sent directly to our IT security team. The team will review the incident, assess its priority, and begin an investigation. You can track the status of your submitted reports in the "My Reports" section of the dashboard.

You can view the real-time status of all major university systems on your main Dashboard. Each service has a visual indicator showing if it's operational, experiencing issues, or currently down.

On the main login page, you will find a "Forgot password?" link. Click on it and follow the on-screen instructions to reset your password securely. An email with a reset link will be sent to your registered university email address.

Yes! The CampusSecure platform is fully responsive and designed to work seamlessly on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

If you have questions that are not covered in these FAQs, please contact the university's IT Help Desk directly at helpdesk@feu.edu.ph or visit them at the IT services office in the main administration building.
